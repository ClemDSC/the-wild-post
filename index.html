organisation

Quests

Through some exercises, "Quests time" is the occasion to discover new concepts on your own, before studying them all together with the trainer.
It is also the time for us to share our knowledges and know-hows, all the exercises being corrected by the students tehmselves - no trainer's correction.

Lessons

A theorical lesson is given by the trainer once a week to explain us the new concepts that were subject of the quests we were working on.

Workshops

Following a lesson, the workshop is a pratical exercise whose aim is to explore a new topic, step by step.

Projects

During the trainee, we are going to work on 3 main projects organised by the trainers board. The purpose is to work in small groups of 4/5 students to build a project as "in real life".
In collaboration with some companies, the last project is based on real customers needs and will finally have to be delivered
